# Prevention Checklist 
 
**DO’s:**

Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on email, social media, and banking apps.
Use strong password (like using different character symbols)
Always verify links before clicking—check spelling and domain names carefully.
Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches and antivirus software.
Log out from shared/public computers after use.Financial / UPI Safety Tips:
Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money—PIN is only required to SEND money.
Do not scan unknown QR codes or accept payment requests from strangers.
Avoid sharing OTP, bank details, or UPI PIN with anyone—even if they claim to be bank officials.

**Do'nt:**

Be cautious of urgent messages offering prizes, jobs, or threats—they are often scams.
Limit personal information shared on social media (phone number, address, college details).
Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or App Store.
  
**Report Cybercrime:**

Report incidents at the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

Call Helpline: 1930 (immediate financial fraud reporting)
