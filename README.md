# 🌐 Digital Literacy Project

Hey there! 👋 Welcome to my Digital Literacy Project. 

In today's hyper-connected world, being online is just a normal part of everyday life. But here's the catch—staying *safe* online is just as important as being connected. I put this project together to spread awareness about digital responsibility, cyber threats, and how we can all practice better internet habits. 

Through real-life scenarios, practical tips, and simple ethical guidelines, my goal here is to help students (and anyone else reading this!) become smarter, safer digital citizens.

---

## 🎯 What I'm Trying to Achieve

Instead of just listing rules, I wanted this project to actually help people:
- Truly understand what digital literacy means in our daily lives.
- Learn how to spot common online threats before they happen—especially tricky ones like UPI fraud and phishing.
- Figure out how to use social media responsibly and positively.
- Learn the basics of ethical digital communication.
- Spread awareness with resources that are actually simple and practical to use.
