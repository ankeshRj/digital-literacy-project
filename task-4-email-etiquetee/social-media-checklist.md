Social Media Do’s and Don’ts 

 DO’s:

Think before posting—ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
Maintain privacy settings and control who can see your post.
Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news.
Use professional language, especially in academic or public discussions.
Respect others’ opinions and engage in healthy, constructive conversations.

DON’Ts:

Don’t share personal information like phone number, address, or passwords.
Don’t post offensive, abusive, or discriminatory content.
Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or online arguments.
Don’t click or share suspicious link or unverified offer.
Don’t overshare your location or daily routine publicly.
